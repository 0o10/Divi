JSON-RPC protocol.

DIVX speaks version 1.0 for maximum compatibility, but uses JSON-RPC 1.1/2.0 standards for parts of the 1.0 standard that were unspecified (HTTP errors and contents of 'error').

1.0 spec: http://json-rpc.org/wiki/specification 1.2 spec: http://jsonrpc.org/historical/json-rpc-over-http.html http://www.codeproject.com/KB/recipes/JSON_Spirit.aspx
